About Me


For the past 10 years I have worked in the service industry as a server, bartender, barback, and bar manager. Before that I had a short stint as an educator after I graduated college with a degree in Microbiology. All of that has led to where I am at today. I have always been fascinated with technology; building computers from a small age, to always keeping up with the newest gaming consoles. Now my time has come to actually enter the field which I enjoy so much, fast forward to today. Currently enrolled in the UCF Coding Bootcamp, which over the course of 3 months I will be exposed to front-end, back-end, and full-stack development, including the following: HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Materialize, AJAX, APIs, Handlebars, Firebase, Node.js, PHP, Laravel, MySQL, MongoDB, Heroku, and Git. I complete the course and will have my certification in July of 2017. I would love to get a head start and find a position before I graduate and sharpen my real world skills.
